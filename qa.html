Frequently asked questions and answers


Take care of yourself, book a comfortable time and a specialist in advance!

On the day of the visit, we will send an SMS notification of the appointment via Whatsapp.
We recommend that you arrive early (15-20 minutes in advance) so that you can slowly and tastefully
immerse yourself in the authentic atmosphere of our spa.


We will take care of everything you need during your stay in our spa complex!

We provide:

  • Robe
  • Towels
  • Disposable underwear for procedures
  • Slippers in individual packaging
It is important that if you decide to visit our fable area, you must have a swimsuit.
Our locker rooms are equipped with hair dryers. You can leave your clothes in comfortable lockers during your stay in our spa.
We kindly ask you to switch the sound of your mobile phones to silent mode and not communicate loudly.
We recommend that you arrive early so that you can slowly change clothes and begin preparing for your program.


Visiting the hammam is contraindicated if:

  • severe form of bronchial asthma;
  • epilepsy;
  • oncological diseases;
  • phlebeurysm;
  • dysfunction of the thyroid gland;
  • if you have heart disease or hypertension, you should not immediately plunge into the pool after a steam room.
It is unacceptable to visit the sauna while under the influence of alcohol or drugs due to the great danger to your health. If you have restrictions or certain contraindications for receiving spa programs or massages, contact your doctor.
We do not provide medical services on site at our spa.


If you decide to reschedule your appointment to another date or time, please notify us no later than two days before your booking

If you are more than 10 minutes late, unfortunately we will not be able to provide a massage or spa program if you have a reserved time with the master, we reserve the right to cancel your appointment

Cancellation of the program in less than 2 hours automatically leads to the debiting of the service from the subscription or gift certificate, if we have not received information from you requesting a transfer

If you do not like the massage technique of the master, you can inform our care manager and he will change the master. It is important to make the request within 10 minutes from the start of the massage or spa program.


Payment is made only in the national currency of the Republic of Kazakhstan.
We accept plastic cards of payment systems Visa, MasterCard, Maestro.
If you have a gift certificate or subscription, it is important that you have it with you.


The validity period of electronic and physical certificates is 3 months from the date of purchase.
The certificate cannot be exchanged or returned.
